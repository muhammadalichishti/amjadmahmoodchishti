About - Amjad

Amjad Mahmood Chishti (Urdu: امجد محمود چشتی), born on April 21, 1972, in the serene village of Chishtian Wala, near Mian Channu in Punjab, Pakistan, is a distinguished writer, satirist, educationist, and social thinker. His prolific career spans over two decades, during which he has established himself as a powerful voice in Urdu literature, education reform, and cultural discourse. With a natural flair for storytelling and satire, Amjad’s works dissect the intricacies of politics, social norms, and educational systems, delivering both humor and thought-provoking critiques.
